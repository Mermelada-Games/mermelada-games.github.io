OUR PROJECTS

Ares Atlas

Is a platformer developed for the 8th Gran CITM Game Jam. Winner of the "Best Technology" award and received two mentions for "best design" and "best game".

Mots

Awarded "Best Fun" and selected for further development by Game BCN and publication by 3Cat.

Mind's Abyss

A captivating 3D cooperative platformer adventure developed for the 7th Gran CITM Game Jam, where players dive into the subconscious realm where a nightmare and a dream must unite to rescue a dying mind. Navigate through surreal landscapes, overcome obstacles, and solve puzzles in this award-winning game. Winner of the "Best Game" award.

Stelar

A serene journey of logic and creativity under stars. In this tranquil 2D puzzle game, dive in the cosmic landscapes as you craft constellations with soothing melodies. With no time pressure, immerse yourself in the wonders of the universe at your own pace. Winner of the esteemed "Premi Ajuntament Terrassa" award and best game with diversifiers.

Sure?

Dive into the fast-paced, cooperative world of Sure, where you and your partner must work together to detect fake news. This game challenges your critical thinking and teamwork skills as you navigate through a series of news articles, social media posts, and other information sources to separate fact from fiction. Developed during the Truths, Lies & Democracy Game Jam, Sure brings together the creativity and expertise of participants from across Europe.

Dit Déu

Step into the divine shoes of the mighty Greek god, Zeus, in this 2D strategy game. Command a mythical army with a tap of your finger and unleash your divine powers to repel enemy hordes. Create your troops, strategically position them, and harness powerful spells to defend Athena in this epic strategic battle. Winner of the "Best Game" award.

ABOUT US

Hello ! We are a passionate group of five game developers currently studying at the Centre de la Imatge i la Tecnologia Multimedia | CITM (UPC). We've developed numerous games for projects and Game Jams in SDL and Unity in 2D, 2.5D, and 3D. Now, we're venturing into Unreal Engine, eager to explore its potential. Join us as we continue to push the boundaries of game development and bring our creative visions to life.
